about me

Hi! I am an electrical engineering student at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign with a strong interest in robotics and human-centered engineering. While robotics is the core of my work, I enjoy approaching problems from multiple disciplines, designing systems that span hardware, software, and user interaction.

My projects include AI and machine learning–based detection systems, bioengineering-inspired robotic systems such as a robotic arm, and web-based interactive technologies. I strongly value versatility in engineering, and I am motivated by projects where technical depth, adaptability, and thoughtful user experience come together. Outside of engineering, I enjoys reading, running, and video editing as a creative outlet beyond technical work.
